ABOUT VFAST HOSTEL

It is part of the BITS Pilani guest house, VFAST is one place where you can find the perfect accommodation. This is one of the most decent and beat place to stay in Pilani. Situated in the campus of BITS Pilani is well maintained.

RULES IN VFAST HOSTEL

  • All electrical appliances should be switched off when not in use.
  • Alcoholic beverages are strictly prohibited.
  • Eatables from outside are not permitted.
  • VFAST HOSTEL does not serve non vegetarian food.
  • Costly belongings should not be kept in the rooms.
  • Washing of clothes is not permitted.
  • Reservations can be cancelled or altered without any prior notice.
  • No tips are to be given to any VFAST HOSTEL worker.
  • Occupants are not supposed to entertain other persons in the VFAST HOSTEL.
